What is a virtual store made in 3D?

A virtual store made in 3D is a digital shopping experience that recreates the look and feel of a physical store using three-dimensional graphics and animations. It allows customers to explore products and interact with the store in a more immersive way than traditional e-commerce websites.

How do I navigate the virtual store?

Navigation is typically done using a mouse or Touching the screen, with the user able to move around the store by clicking and dragging. Some virtual stores may also allow for keyboard navigation.

Can I interact with products in the virtual store?

Yes, many virtual stores allow for product interaction such as zooming in, rotating, and viewing from different angles. Some virtual stores may even allow for product customization, such as changing colors or adding accessories.
